French Onion Soup

Ingredients

  • 6 large yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup dry white wine
  • 8 cups beef broth
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 baguette, sliced
  • 2 cups grated Gruyère cheese

Instructions

  1. Melt butter in a large pot over medium heat. Add onions and cook until caramelized, stirring occasionally, about 25-30 minutes.
  2. Add garlic and cook for 1-2 minutes, until fragrant.
  3. Stir in wine and bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer for 10 minutes, until the wine has reduced.
  4. Add beef broth, bay leaves, thyme, salt, and pepper. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 30 minutes.
  5. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Arrange baguette slices on a baking sheet and toast for 5 minutes, until crisp.
  6. Set oven to broil. Ladle soup into oven-safe bowls, top with toasted baguette slices, and sprinkle with Gruyère cheese.
  7. Place bowls on a baking sheet and broil until cheese is melted and bubbly, about 2-4 minutes. Serve hot.
